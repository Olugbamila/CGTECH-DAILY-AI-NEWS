### Walnut Coding Brings AI Education to Schools through Public Welfare Programming Courses
Source: The Manila Times

Summary:
Walnut Coding, a leading online coding platform, is promoting artificial intelligence (AI) education in primary and secondary schools across China. The company's AI education courses have already reached around 140,000 students in 1,029 schools in Shanghai, and have also provided training to over 50,000 teachers nationwide. The initiative aims to integrate AI education into classrooms by providing curriculum materials, teacher training, and hands-on learning tools, benefiting students and teachers across the country.

### UK Supreme Court ruling on patents and AI is boost for innovation, lawyers say
Source: The Star

Summary:
The UK Supreme Court has ruled that artificial neural networks, a form of artificial intelligence, can be patented. This decision is expected to boost the patenting of AI innovations, according to lawyers. The ruling, made on Wednesday, allows for the protection of intellectual property related to machine learning and artificial intelligence technologies.
