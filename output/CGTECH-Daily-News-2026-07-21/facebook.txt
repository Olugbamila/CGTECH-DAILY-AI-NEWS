Source: PLOS (Public Library of Science)
Link: https://journals.plos.org/digitalhealth/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pdig.0001486

POST:
Did you know that chronic kidney disease (CKD) often has no early symptoms, putting millions of people at risk? That's why the development of MERWACS, a machine learning tool that identifies individuals at risk of CKD, is such a game-changer. By using easily measurable factors like age and blood pressure, MERWACS provides a personalized predicted probability of reduced kidney function.
