Source: Devdiscourse
URL: https://www.devdiscourse.com/article/law-order/3915117-cag-taps-artificial-intelligence-machine-learning-to-improve-public-auditing-quality

SUMMARY:
The Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG) of India is leveraging artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) to enhance the quality, speed, and depth of public auditing. This initiative aims to improve the overall efficiency and effectiveness of the auditing process. The use of AI and ML is expected to have a significant impact on the public auditing sector in India.
