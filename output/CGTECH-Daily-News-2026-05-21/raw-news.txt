Source: Devdiscourse
URL: https://www.devdiscourse.com/article/law-order/3915117-cag-taps-artificial-intelligence-machine-learning-to-improve-public-auditing-quality

SUMMARY:
India's apex auditor, the Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG), is developing a large language model platform to enhance public auditing quality, speed, and depth. This platform will utilize artificial intelligence and machine learning to identify procurement concentration and cartel-risk indicators in real-time. The goal is to improve the overall efficiency and effectiveness of public auditing. By leveraging AI and ML, CAG aims to strengthen its auditing capabilities and provide more accurate insights. This development is a significant step towards modernizing the auditing process in India.
