ARTICLE 4: AI-Powered Breakthrough: GlaucoXAI Detects Early Glaucoma with Unprecedented Accuracy
Source: PLOS (Public Library of Science)
URL: https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pcbi.1013139

SUMMARY:
Researchers have developed GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that can detect early glaucoma from retinal fundus images with over 93-95% accuracy. The model uses a hybrid approach combining feature extraction, dimensionality reduction, and optimized neural learning. GlaucoXAI generates visual explanations that highlight regions of the eye most responsible for its predictions, supporting ophthalmologists in verifying AI-driven results. This breakthrough demonstrates that AI can be both accurate and interpretable, marking a step toward reliable clinical tools for early glaucoma diagnosis. The model has the potential to improve patient care by enabling early detection and treatment of glaucoma.
