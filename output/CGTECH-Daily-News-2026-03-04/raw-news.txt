Source: The Economic Times
URL: https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/tech/artificial-intelligence/texas-at-heart-of-amazons-ai-push-in-united-states/articleshow/128838396.cms

SUMMARY:
Amazon is making a significant push into artificial intelligence (AI) in the United States, with a focus on Texas. This development is expected to have a major impact on the tech industry and AI advancements.

Amazon's making a big move in AI, designing its own custom chips for machine learning. This could change the game for the tech giant and the industry as a whole.
