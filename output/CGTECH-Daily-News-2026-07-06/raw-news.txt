URL: https://www.naturalnews.com/2026-07-06-drones-use-machine-learning-predict-mechanical-failures.html

SUMMARY:

Source: Phys.org
URL: https://phys.org/news/2026-06-product-ai-approach.html

SUMMARY:
A new AI approach is being developed to assess learning processes, rather than just final products, in order to truly understand whether students are learning. This method aims to evaluate the steps students take to arrive at an answer, rather than just the answer itself. By doing so, educators can gain a more comprehensive understanding of student learning and identify areas where students may need additional support. The approach has the potential to revolutionize the way we assess student learning and provide more accurate feedback. This shift in focus from product to process can help to ensure that students are developing the critical thinking and problem-solving skills they need to succeed.
