Source: Medical Xpress
URL: https://medicalxpress.com/news/2026-06-language-based-ai-early-heart.html

SUMMARY:
A recent study published in the International Journal of Medical Engineering and Informatics has shown that a language-based AI model can detect early heart disease in ECGs with an accuracy of 94.2%. The model, based on Transformer architecture, was originally developed for language processing but has been adapted for medical use. This breakthrough has the potential to revolutionize the diagnosis and treatment of heart disease. The model was tested on several well-known medical datasets and showed promising results.
