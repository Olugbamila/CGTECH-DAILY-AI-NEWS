Source: The Tribune
URL: https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/business/star-health-and-amplify-health-partner-to-scale-ai-led-claims-intelligence-across-indias-largest-private-health-insurance-operations/

SUMMARY:
Star Health and Allied Insurance Company Limited, India's largest standalone health insurer, has partnered with Amplify Health to deploy AI-led claims intelligence and healthcare analytics capabilities across its operations. This three-year partnership aims to enhance customer experience by making the cashless claims journey faster, more predictable, and less stressful. Amplify Health will bring its AI and analytics capabilities, refined across 10 Asian markets, to India. The partnership is part of Star Health's push to invest in the digital health stack, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, and predictive analytics. This move is expected to benefit Star Health's customers and improve the overall healthcare experience.
