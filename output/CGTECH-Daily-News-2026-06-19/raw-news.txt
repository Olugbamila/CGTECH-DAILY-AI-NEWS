ARTICLE 3: AI Model Detects Early Heart Disease in ECGs with 94.2% Accuracy
Source: Medical Xpress
URL: https://medicalxpress.com/news/2026-06-language-based-ai-early-heart.html

SUMMARY:
Researchers have developed a language-based AI model that can detect early heart disease in electrocardiograms (ECGs) with 94.2% accuracy. The model, based on Transformer architecture, was originally developed for language processing but has been adapted for medical use. Tests have shown that it works well with data from several well-known medical datasets. This breakthrough has the potential to revolutionize the way heart disease is diagnosed and treated. The model's high accuracy could lead to earlier interventions and better patient outcomes.
