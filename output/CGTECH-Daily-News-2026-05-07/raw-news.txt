ARTICLE 2: GlaucoXAI: The AI-Powered Glaucoma Detection Model
Source: PLOS (Public Library of Science)
URL: https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pcbi.1013139

SUMMARY:
Researchers have developed GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that detects early glaucoma from retinal fundus images with over 93-95% accuracy. The model uses a hybrid approach combining feature extraction, dimensionality reduction, and optimized neural learning. GlaucoXAI generates visual explanations that highlight regions of the eye most responsible for its predictions, supporting ophthalmologists in verifying AI-driven results. This innovation marks a significant step towards reliable clinical tools for early glaucoma diagnosis and better patient care. The study's findings were published in the Public Library of Science (PLOS) journal.

SHORT-FORM SCRIPT:
Imagine a world where glaucoma is diagnosed early, thanks to AI. Meet GlaucoXAI, a new explainable AI model that detects glaucoma from retinal images with high accuracy and provides visual explanations for its predictions.
