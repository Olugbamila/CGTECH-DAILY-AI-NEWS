ARTICLE 5: AI Adoption Accelerates in Finance, But Capabilities Lag Behind
Source: The Manila Times
URL: https://www.manilatimes.net/2026/04/17/tmt-newswire/pr-newswire/ai-adoption-accelerates-in-finance-but-capabilities-are-lagging-behind/2322518

SUMMARY:
A recent survey found that 50% of financial services sector business leaders have deployed AI in their organizations, but only in limited departments or functions. The main barriers to scaling AI adoption include data governance and privacy compliance, cybersecurity and risk concerns, and shortages in AI talent and technical expertise. Finance leaders identify AI and machine learning expertise as the most critical capability to develop, yet it is also cited as the most lacking. Additionally, 42% of leaders report difficulty attracting talent with emerging skills such as AI, ESG, and cybersecurity. The survey highlights the need for financial institutions to invest in workforce capabilities to keep pace with AI adoption.
