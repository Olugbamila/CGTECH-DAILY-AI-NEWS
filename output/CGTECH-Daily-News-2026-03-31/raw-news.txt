Source: Phys.org
URL: https://phys.org/news/2026-03-college-students-ai-theyre-simply.html

SUMMARY:
A recent pilot study examines how college students use generative AI for writing, revealing that they are not simply relying on AI to write for them. Instead, students are using AI as a tool to support their learning, raising questions about the potential benefits and drawbacks of AI in higher education.
