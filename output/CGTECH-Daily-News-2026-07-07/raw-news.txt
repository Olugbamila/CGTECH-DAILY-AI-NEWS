ARTICLE 1: Delhi High Court Protects Ravi Kishan's Personality Rights from AI Misuse
Source: Deccan Chronicle
URL: https://www.deccanchronicle.com/entertainment/bollywood/delhi-high-court-protects-ravi-kishans-personality-rights-restrains-ai-and-deepfake-misuse-1968963

SUMMARY:
The Delhi High Court has granted an ex parte ad interim injunction in favour of actor and BJP MP Ravi Kishan, restraining the unauthorised use of his name, image, voice, and other personality attributes for commercial or personal gain through any technology, including Artificial Intelligence, Generative AI, Machine Learning, and Deepfakes. This move aims to protect Ravi Kishan's personality rights and prevent misuse. The court's decision highlights the growing concern over AI and deepfake misuse. The injunction applies across any medium, ensuring Ravi Kishan's public persona attributes are not misappropriated.

The event showcased the potential of AI to augment education and prepare children for the future.
