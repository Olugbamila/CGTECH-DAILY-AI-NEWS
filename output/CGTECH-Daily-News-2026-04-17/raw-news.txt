ARTICLE 5: AI vs ML vs DL: A Complete Comparison Guide
Source: TechBullion
URL: https://techbullion.com/artificial-intelligence-vs-machine-learning-vs-deep-learning-a-complete-comparison-guide/

SUMMARY:
Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Deep Learning (DL) are often used interchangeably, but they have distinct differences. AI refers to the broader field of research and development aimed at creating machines that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. ML is a subset of AI that involves training algorithms to learn from data, while DL is a subset of ML that uses neural networks to analyze data. Understanding the differences between these technologies is crucial for businesses and individuals looking to leverage their power. The guide provides a comprehensive comparison of AI, ML, and DL, including their key differences, use cases, and applications in modern AI systems.
