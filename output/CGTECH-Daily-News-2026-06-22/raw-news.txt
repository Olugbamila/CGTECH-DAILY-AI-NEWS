Source: Medical Xpress
URL: https://medicalxpress.com/news/2026-06-language-based-ai-early-heart.html

SUMMARY:
Researchers have developed a language-based AI model that can detect early heart disease in electrocardiograms (ECGs) with 94.2% accuracy. The model, based on Transformer architecture, was originally developed for language processing but has been adapted for medical use. Tests have shown the model to be effective with data from several well-known medical datasets. This breakthrough has the potential to revolutionize the early detection and treatment of heart disease. The model's high accuracy could lead to improved patient outcomes and reduced healthcare costs.
