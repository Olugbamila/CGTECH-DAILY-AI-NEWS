ARTICLE 2: Revolutionizing Glaucoma Diagnosis with Explainable AI
Source: PLOS (Public Library of Science)
URL: https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pcbi.1013139

SUMMARY:
Researchers have developed GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that can detect glaucoma early from retinal fundus images with over 93-95% accuracy. The model uses a hybrid approach combining feature extraction, dimensionality reduction, and optimized neural learning. GlaucoXAI generates visual explanations that highlight regions of the eye most responsible for its predictions, making it a reliable clinical tool for early glaucoma diagnosis. This breakthrough demonstrates that AI can be both accurate and interpretable, marking a significant step toward better patient care. The model has the potential to revolutionize the field of ophthalmology by providing clinicians with a trustworthy and transparent tool for diagnosing glaucoma.

SHORT-FORM SCRIPT:
Imagine a world where glaucoma can be detected early, saving millions from irreversible blindness. Introducing GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that's making it possible with over 93% accuracy.
