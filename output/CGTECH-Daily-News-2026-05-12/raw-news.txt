ARTICLE 3: Revolutionizing Glaucoma Detection: Explainable AI Model Achieves 93% Accuracy
Source: PLOS (Public Library of Science)
URL: https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pcbi.1013139

SUMMARY:
Researchers have developed GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that detects glaucoma early from retinal fundus images with over 93-95% accuracy. This hybrid approach integrates feature extraction, dimensionality reduction, and optimized neural learning, making its predictions more transparent and trustworthy to clinicians. GlaucoXAI generates visual explanations that highlight regions of the eye most responsible for its predictions, supporting ophthalmologists in verifying AI-driven results. The model has been tested on two publicly available datasets and outperforms existing models. This breakthrough demonstrates the potential of AI in reliable clinical tools for early glaucoma diagnosis and better patient care.

SHORT-FORM SCRIPT:
Imagine a world where glaucoma is detected early, saving millions from irreversible blindness. Introducing GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that's making it possible with over 93% accuracy.
