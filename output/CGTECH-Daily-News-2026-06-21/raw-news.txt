Source: Medical Xpress
URL: https://medicalxpress.com/news/2026-06-language-based-ai-early-heart.html

SUMMARY:
Researchers have developed a machine-learning model based on Transformer architecture that can detect early heart disease in electrocardiograms (ECGs) with 94.2% accuracy. This model, originally designed for language processing, has shown promising results in medical diagnostics. The study, published in the International Journal of Medical Engineering and Informatics, demonstrates the potential of AI in healthcare. The model was tested on several well-known medical datasets, showcasing its effectiveness. This breakthrough could lead to earlier detection and treatment of heart disease, improving patient outcomes.
