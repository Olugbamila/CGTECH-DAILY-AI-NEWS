Source: PLOS (Public Library of Science)
Link: https://journals.plos.org/digitalhealth/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pdig.0001486

POST:
Did you know that chronic kidney disease (CKD) often has no early symptoms, putting millions of people at risk? That's why researchers have developed MERWACS, a non-invasive machine learning tool that can help identify individuals who should undergo CKD screening. By using factors like age, weight, blood pressure, and medical history, MERWACS provides a personalized predicted probability of reduced kidney function.
