ARTICLE 3: AI Model Detects Early Heart Disease in ECGs with 94.2% Accuracy
Source: Medical Xpress
URL: https://medicalxpress.com/news/2026-06-language-based-ai-early-heart.html

SUMMARY:
Researchers have developed a language-based AI model that can detect early heart disease in electrocardiograms (ECGs) with 94.2% accuracy. The model, based on the Transformer architecture, was originally designed for language processing but has been adapted for medical use. Tests have shown the model to be effective with data from several well-known medical datasets. This breakthrough has the potential to revolutionize the early detection and treatment of heart disease. The study was published in the International Journal of Medical Engineering and Informatics.

SHORT-FORM SCRIPT:
Imagine a world where AI can detect heart disease before it's too late. A new language-based AI model can analyze ECGs with 94.2% accuracy, giving doctors a powerful tool in the fight against heart disease.
