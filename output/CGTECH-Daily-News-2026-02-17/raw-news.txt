ARTICLE 5: India to Roll Out AI-Powered Customs Clearance Scheme by March 1
Source: NDTV Profit
URL: https://www.ndtvprofit.com/economy/cbic-to-roll-out-eligible-manufacturer-importer-scheme-by-march-1-chairman-10996275

SUMMARY:
The Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs (CBIC) is set to introduce the Eligible Manufacturer-Importer scheme by March 1. This new system aims to reduce physical inspections and human intervention by leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning in risk assessment and cargo screening. The draft framework will be released in five to six days for stakeholder consultation. The goal is to enable faster, data-driven decision-making. This move is expected to increase efficiency and reduce delays in the customs clearance process.

SHORT-FORM SCRIPT:
India's customs board is rolling out a new scheme that uses AI and machine learning to speed up cargo screening and reduce physical inspections. This could mean faster clearance times and less human intervention.
