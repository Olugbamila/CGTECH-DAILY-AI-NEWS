ARTICLE 5: India's Customs Department to Introduce AI-Powered Cargo Screening Scheme
Source: NDTV Profit
URL: https://www.ndtvprofit.com/economy/cbic-to-roll-out-eligible-manufacturer-importer-scheme-by-march-1-chairman-10996275

SUMMARY:
The Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs (CBIC) is set to introduce the Eligible Manufacturer-Importer scheme by March 1, aiming to reduce physical inspections and human intervention in cargo screening. The new system will leverage artificial intelligence and machine learning for risk assessment and data-driven decision-making. A draft framework will be released for stakeholder consultation within five to six days. This move is expected to increase efficiency and speed in cargo processing. The CBIC chairman announced the rollout plan, highlighting the importance of technology in modernizing customs procedures.

India's customs department is about to get a major tech boost. The CBIC is introducing a new scheme that uses AI and machine learning to screen cargo, reducing the need for physical inspections and human intervention.
