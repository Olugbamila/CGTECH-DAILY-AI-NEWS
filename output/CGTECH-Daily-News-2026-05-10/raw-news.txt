ARTICLE 2: Explainable AI Model Detects Glaucoma with Unprecedented Accuracy
Source: PLOS (Public Library of Science)
URL: https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pcbi.1013139

SUMMARY:
Researchers have developed GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that can detect glaucoma early from retinal fundus images with over 93-95% accuracy. The model uses a hybrid approach combining feature extraction, dimensionality reduction, and optimized neural learning to enhance accuracy and speed. GlaucoXAI generates visual explanations that highlight regions of the eye most responsible for its predictions, supporting ophthalmologists in verifying AI-driven results. This breakthrough marks a step toward reliable clinical tools for early glaucoma diagnosis and better patient care. The study was published in the Public Library of Science journal.

SHORT-FORM SCRIPT:
Imagine a world where glaucoma can be detected early, saving millions from irreversible blindness. Meet GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that's making it possible with over 93% accuracy.
