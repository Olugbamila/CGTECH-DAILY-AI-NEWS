ARTICLE 2: GlaucoXAI: The Explainable AI Model Revolutionizing Glaucoma Detection
Source: PLOS (Public Library of Science)
URL: https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pcbi.1013139

SUMMARY:
Researchers have developed GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that detects early glaucoma from retinal fundus images with over 93-95% accuracy. The model uses a hybrid approach, combining feature extraction, dimensionality reduction, and optimized neural learning. GlaucoXAI generates visual explanations, highlighting regions of the eye responsible for its predictions, making it a reliable clinical tool for early glaucoma diagnosis. This breakthrough demonstrates that AI can be both accurate and interpretable, supporting ophthalmologists in verifying AI-driven results. The model has the potential to improve patient care by enabling early detection and treatment of glaucoma.

SHORT-FORM SCRIPT:
Imagine a world where glaucoma is detected early, saving millions from irreversible blindness. Introducing GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that's making it possible with over 93% accuracy.
