The model combines advanced image analysis with explainable AI methods, making its predictions transparent and trustworthy to clinicians. GlaucoXAI outperforms existing models and generates visual explanations to support ophthalmologists in verifying AI-driven results. This innovation marks a step toward reliable clinical tools for early glaucoma diagnosis and better patient care. The study was published in the Public Library of Science journal.
