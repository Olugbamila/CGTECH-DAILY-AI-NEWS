ARTICLE 5: AI Model Detects Heart Disease in ECGs with 94.2% Accuracy
Source: Medical Xpress
URL: https://medicalxpress.com/news/2026-06-language-based-ai-early-heart.html

SUMMARY:
Researchers have developed a language-based AI model that can detect early heart disease in ECGs with 94.2% accuracy. The model, based on Transformer architecture, was originally designed for language processing but has been adapted for medical use. Tests have shown its effectiveness with data from several well-known medical datasets. This breakthrough has the potential to improve early detection and treatment of heart disease. The model's high accuracy could lead to better patient outcomes and reduced healthcare costs.

SHORT-FORM SCRIPT:
Imagine an AI that can detect heart disease from just an ECG. Researchers have created a language-based model that does just that, with 94.2% accuracy. This could revolutionize early detection and treatment.
