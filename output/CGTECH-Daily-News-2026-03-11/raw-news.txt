ARTICLE 2: The Transformer Revolution: How a Single Architecture Changed the Face of AI
Source: The Economic Times
URL: https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/international/us/what-is-a-transformer-in-artificial-intelligence-and-why-is-it-the-base-of-most-modern-ai-models/articleshow/129397230.cms

SUMMARY:
The Transformer architecture, introduced by Google researchers in 2017, has revolutionized machine learning and powers over 90% of modern AI models. It enables AI models to understand context, analyze large datasets, and predict words using probability. This design is the foundation of large language models like ChatGPT, which rely on trillions of text tokens and human feedback for training. The Transformer's impact is evident in its dominance of generative AI, search, and automation. Its influence can be seen in various applications, from language translation to text summarization.

Imagine a technology that can understand context, analyze massive datasets, and predict words with precision. This is the power of the Transformer architecture, introduced by Google in 2017. It's the backbone of modern AI models, including ChatGPT, and has changed the game for machine learning.
