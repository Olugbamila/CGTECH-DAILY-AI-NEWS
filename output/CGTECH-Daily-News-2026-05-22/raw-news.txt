ARTICLE 1: India's CAG Taps AI to Improve Public Auditing Quality
Source: Devdiscourse
URL: https://www.devdiscourse.com/article/law-order/3915117-cag-taps-artificial-intelligence-machine-learning-to-improve-public-auditing-quality

SUMMARY:
India's Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG) is leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning to enhance the quality, speed, and depth of public auditing. The CAG is developing a large language model platform to achieve this goal. This platform will enable real-time identification of procurement concentration and cartel-risk indicators, improving the overall auditing process. The use of AI and ML is expected to significantly improve the efficiency and effectiveness of public auditing in India. This development highlights the growing importance of AI in the auditing sector.
