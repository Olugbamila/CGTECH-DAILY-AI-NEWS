ARTICLE 4: Artificial Intelligence vs Machine Learning vs Deep Learning: A Complete Comparison Guide
Source: TechBullion
URL: https://techbullion.com/artificial-intelligence-vs-machine-learning-vs-deep-learning-a-complete-comparison-guide/

SUMMARY:
Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Deep Learning (DL) are often used interchangeably, but they have distinct differences. AI refers to the broader field of research and development aimed at creating machines that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. ML is a subset of AI that involves training algorithms to learn from data, while DL is a subset of ML that uses neural networks to analyze data. Understanding the differences between these technologies is crucial for developing and implementing effective AI systems.
