ARTICLE 1: Revolutionizing Healthcare: Star Health Partners with Amplify Health to Deploy AI-Led Claims Intelligence
Source: The Tribune
URL: https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/business/star-health-and-amplify-health-partner-to-scale-ai-led-claims-intelligence-across-indias-largest-private-health-insurance-operations/

SUMMARY:
Star Health and Allied Insurance Company Limited, India's largest standalone health insurer, has partnered with Amplify Health to deploy AI-led claims intelligence and healthcare analytics capabilities across its health insurance operations. This partnership aims to enhance customer experience by making the cashless claims journey faster, more predictable, and less stressful for customers during hospitalization. The three-year partnership will leverage Amplify Health's AI and analytics capabilities, which have been refined across 10 Asian markets. This move is part of Star Health's broader push to invest in the digital health stack, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, and predictive analytics. The goal is to improve the overall claims settlement process and provide a better experience for policyholders.

ARTICLE 4: Revolutionizing Assessment: AI Approach Evaluates Learning Processes
Source: Phys.org
URL: https://phys.org/news/2026-06-product-ai-approach.html

SUMMARY:
A new AI approach is being developed to assess learning processes, rather than just final products, in order to truly understand whether students are learning. This is in response to the increasing use of AI tools in education, which can make it difficult for educators to determine whether students are genuinely learning or just producing polished answers with AI assistance. The goal of this approach is to provide a more comprehensive understanding of student learning. By focusing on the process, educators can identify areas where students need extra support and tailor their instruction accordingly. This shift in assessment practices has the potential to revolutionize the way we evaluate student learning.

SHORT-FORM SCRIPT:
Imagine a classroom where AI helps students learn, but doesn't do the work for them. A new AI approach is changing how we assess learning, focusing on the process, not just the final product.
