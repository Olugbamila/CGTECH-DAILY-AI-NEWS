ARTICLE 4: Revolutionizing Glaucoma Detection: The Power of Explainable AI
Source: PLOS (Public Library of Science)
URL: https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pcbi.1013139

SUMMARY:
Researchers have developed GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that detects glaucoma early from retinal fundus images with over 93-95% accuracy. The model uses a hybrid approach integrating feature extraction, dimensionality reduction, and optimized neural learning to enhance accuracy and speed. GlaucoXAI generates visual explanations that highlight regions of the eye most responsible for its predictions, supporting ophthalmologists in verifying AI-driven results. This work demonstrates that AI can be both accurate and interpretable, marking a step toward reliable clinical tools for early glaucoma diagnosis and better patient care. The model was tested on two publicly available datasets and outperformed existing models.

SHORT-FORM SCRIPT:
Imagine a world where glaucoma is detected early, saving millions from irreversible blindness. Meet GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that's making it possible with over 93% accuracy.
