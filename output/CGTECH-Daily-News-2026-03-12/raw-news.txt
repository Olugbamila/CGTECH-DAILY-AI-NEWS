ARTICLE 3: The Power of Transformer Architecture: The Backbone of Modern AI
Source: The Economic Times
URL: https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/international/us/what-is-a-transformer-in-artificial-intelligence-and-why-is-it-the-base-of-most-modern-ai-models/articleshow/129397230.cms

SUMMARY:
The Transformer architecture, introduced by Google researchers in 2017, has revolutionized machine learning and powers over 90% of modern AI models. It enables AI models to understand context, analyze large datasets, and predict words using probability. This design is the foundation of large language models like ChatGPT, which dominate generative AI, search, and automation. The Transformer's impact is a result of its ability to train on trillions of text tokens and human feedback. As a result, Transformer-based models have become the standard in the AI industry.

SHORT-FORM SCRIPT:
Imagine a technology that can understand context, analyze massive datasets, and predict words with precision. This is the power of the Transformer architecture, introduced by Google in 2017. It's the backbone of modern AI models, including ChatGPT, and has changed the game for generative AI, search, and automation.
