Dr. Tarandeep Singh Gill, a surgeon from Chandigarh, has successfully performed his 3,000th knee surgery using AI-powered RoboLens technology, a national first in India. The RoboLens platform integrates AI, machine learning algorithms, and augmented reality to create a 3D virtual model of each patient's knee before surgery. This milestone makes Tricity a global benchmark for AI-assisted orthopedic surgery. Dr. Gill's achievement is a testament to the potential of AI in revolutionizing healthcare. The use of AI-powered technology has improved the accuracy and efficiency of knee surgeries, benefiting thousands of patients.
