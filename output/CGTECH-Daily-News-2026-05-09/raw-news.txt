ARTICLE 2: Revolutionizing Glaucoma Detection: The Power of Explainable AI
Source: PLOS (Public Library of Science)
URL: https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pcbi.1013139

SUMMARY:
Researchers have developed GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that detects glaucoma early from retinal fundus images with over 93-95% accuracy. The model combines advanced image analysis with explainable AI methods, making its predictions transparent and trustworthy to clinicians. GlaucoXAI outperforms existing models and generates visual explanations to support ophthalmologists in verifying AI-driven results. This innovation marks a step toward reliable clinical tools for early glaucoma diagnosis and better patient care. The model's hybrid approach integrates feature extraction, dimensionality reduction, and optimized neural learning to enhance accuracy and speed.

SHORT-FORM SCRIPT:
Imagine a world where glaucoma is detected early, saving millions from irreversible blindness. Introducing GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that's making it possible with over 93% accuracy.
