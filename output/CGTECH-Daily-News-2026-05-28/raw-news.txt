ARTICLE 2: India's Apex Auditor Leverages AI to Enhance Public Auditing Quality
Source: Devdiscourse
URL: https://www.devdiscourse.com/article/law-order/3915117-cag-taps-artificial-intelligence-machine-learning-to-improve-public-auditing-quality

SUMMARY:
India's apex auditor, the Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG), is leveraging artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) to enhance public auditing quality, speed, and depth. The CAG is developing a large language model platform to achieve this goal. This platform will enable the real-time identification of procurement concentration and cartel-risk indicators, improving the overall auditing process. The use of AI and ML is expected to significantly improve the quality and efficiency of public auditing in India. This move highlights the growing importance of AI in the auditing sector.
