ARTICLE 2: Revolutionizing Glaucoma Detection: The Power of Explainable AI
Source: PLOS (Public Library of Science)
URL: https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pcbi.1013139

SUMMARY:
Researchers have developed GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that detects glaucoma early from retinal fundus images with over 93-95% accuracy. The model combines advanced image analysis with explainable AI methods, making its predictions more transparent and trustworthy to clinicians. GlaucoXAI generates visual explanations that highlight regions of the eye most responsible for its predictions, supporting ophthalmologists in verifying AI-driven results. This innovation marks a step toward reliable clinical tools for early glaucoma diagnosis and better patient care. The study demonstrates that AI can be both accurate and interpretable.

SHORT-FORM SCRIPT:
Imagine a world where glaucoma is detected early, saving millions from irreversible blindness. Introducing GlaucoXAI, an explainable AI model that's making it possible with over 93% accuracy.
