ARTICLE 1: India's Apex Auditor Leverages AI to Enhance Public Auditing Quality
Source: Devdiscourse
URL: https://www.devdiscourse.com/article/law-order/3915117-cag-taps-artificial-intelligence-machine-learning-to-improve-public-auditing-quality

SUMMARY:
India's apex auditor, the Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG), is leveraging artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) to enhance public auditing quality, speed, and depth. The CAG is developing a large language model platform to achieve this goal. The platform aims to identify procurement concentration and cartel-risk indicators in real-time, improving the overall auditing process. This move is expected to significantly improve the quality and efficiency of public audits in India. By adopting AI and ML, the CAG is taking a proactive approach to ensuring transparency and accountability in public spending.
